How do I download a file?

First, click one of the buttons above. After this, to actually download the file, you may need to right-click (or long-press on mobile) and choose "Save Video As..." (or "Save Page As..."). On mobile devices, you may need to open the player menu (the ...-dots) and tap "Download".
